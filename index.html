About

Jeisson David Apolo Armas

Jason was born in Zamora, Ecuador, a town of two thousand people in the Amazon basin. He is a recent graduate from Cornell University with both a Bachelor of Architecture with a concentration in Architectural Theory and a Bachelor of Science in Urban and Regional Studies.

Jason has worked at Landon Bone Baker Architects in Chicago, IL; Adrian Smith and Gordon Gill Architecture in Chicago, IL; Rafael Vinoly Architects in New York, NY; Cayuga Waterfront Trail Initiative in Ithaca, NY; Friends of Stewart Park in Ithaca, NY; and Wharton Studio Museum in Ithaca, NY.

Jason can be reached at jasonapolo@gmail.com
